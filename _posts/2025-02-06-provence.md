## Arrival in Nice (Dec 21)
Everyone in Toulon (and Marseille) had been so accommodating, patiently speaking French with me. I was pleasantly surprised by this, and the contrast was stark when I arrived in Nice that evening. The first two restaurants I entered, the waiters responded rudly in English, "no, full," without replying to my "bon soir." The third restaurant had space, but after being told to sit anywhere, I was ignored. The waiters were fooling around, so I walked out. Thankfully, the rest of my experience in Nice was good. I found a good place to eat and write in peace. After dinner, I visited the Christmas market for some mulled wine. My flight scheduled for 21h45‚Äì22h45 was pushed back to 23h30‚Äì00h30, 30 minutes after the last train left Geneva Airport.
